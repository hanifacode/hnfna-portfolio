Techcomfest WDC 2023

Home Activities Blog Single

Discovering Talents Through A Competition Event.

Techcomfest is an IT competition organized by the Semarang State Polytechnic.

Features various competition categories, such as ctf, web design, and more. This was my first experience participating in an IT competition, and I made it to the finals, securing the second place. My team and I took part in the web design competition without backend development. Together with my classmate, Adinda Slavina, we worked on building the website from UI/UX design, creating a prototype, and finally preparing the website for the competition.

In the first round, the top 10 participants were selected to advance to the next round. The subsequent round consisted of presentations and question-and-answer sessions, with the ultimate decision on the winners resting in the hands of the judges.
